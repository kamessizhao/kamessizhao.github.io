About

I am a 4th-year PhD student at the Operations Research Center at MIT, where I am advised by Prof. Daniel Freund. Prior to my PhD, I graduated Summa Cum Laude from Columbia University in 2020 with a B.S. degree in Operations Research: Financial Engineering.

My research focuses on platform operations and market design problems in online marketplaces. In tackling these challenges, my works lie at the intersection of online algorithms, stochastic decision-making, and game theory. In summer 2023, I worked at UberEats Realtime Pricing Team as an applied scientist PhD intern. Here is my Curriculum Vitae (CV).
